Design System

The single source of truth for our visual language. This system translates our core philosophy into a tangible, consistent, and evocative user experience.

Principles

Human & Evocative

Prioritize warmth and organic feeling. The design should feel like it was made by people, for people.

Clear & Intentional

Every element has a purpose. We avoid decoration for its own sake, ensuring clarity and focus.

Spacious & Rhythmic

Use generous spacing to create calm and allow the "relational space" to breathe.

Spacing & Rhythm
